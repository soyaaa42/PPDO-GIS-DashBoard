These recommendations guide local planning priorities using the sector-based dashboard structure and CBMS infrastructure indicators.

Economic Sector

Promote livelihood programs, support local enterprises, improve market access, and provide skills training to increase employment opportunities and household income.

Infrastructure Sector

Prioritize water supply, toilet facilities, electricity access, road improvement, drainage systems, and public facilities in areas with lower service coverage.

Social Sector

🏛️

Institutional Sector

Improve digital records, barangay coordination, disaster preparedness, transparency, and citizen feedback systems for faster and more responsive governance.
