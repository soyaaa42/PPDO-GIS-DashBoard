Planning Analysis

Social Sector Planning Recommendation

Use this as the interpretation block for your dashboard.

Health

Prioritize accessible barangay-level health services, medicine availability, and referral systems for communities far from major facilities.

Education

Support school-age children through scholarship, transportation, learning recovery, and dropout-prevention programs.

Senior Citizens

Update senior citizen records, improve ID registration, monitor social pension eligibility, and provide regular health and wellness activities.

Youth and Welfare

Strengthen youth skills training, livelihood pathways, family assistance validation, and protection services for vulnerable groups.

Recommended Priority Action

  • Build a unified social services database per municipality and barangay.
  • Connect CBMS indicators with health, education, senior citizen, youth, and welfare programs.
  • Use GIS mapping to identify underserved barangays and prioritize intervention.
  • Schedule quarterly monitoring for beneficiaries and service gaps.
